About Social Action Uganda

Social Action Uganda is a community-based organization operating in Wakiso District since 2024. We are registered under the Wakiso District Community-Based Organisation framework, Registration No: WCBO/24/4122.

Mission: We work with communities, families, local leaders, faith institutions, and young people to prevent child marriage, protect vulnerable children, promote human rights, support vulnerable people through charity, and strengthen communities through awareness and emergency response.

Our Programs

1. Ending Child Marriage

We protect and empower girls and boys through education, awareness campaigns, and community engagement to prevent child marriage in Wakiso District.

2. Emergency Relief

We provide immediate support to families affected by crisis, delivering food, supplies, and essential services to vulnerable communities.

3. Human Rights Advocacy

We promote and defend human rights through education, legal awareness, and community dialogue.

4. Women & Youth Empowerment

We equip women and youth with skills, resources, and opportunities for economic independence and leadership.
